What is a TDEE Calculator?

TDEE stands for Total Daily Energy Expenditure, and provides you with the amount of calories you would have to consume to maintain your current weight. It can be used as a guideance in your nutrition to help you gain, lose, or maintain a certain weight.

How is it Calculated?

The TDEE calculator takes in a variety of factors such as age, sex, weight, and height to combine your basic metabolic rate (BMR) and your activitiy level to calculate an estimate on how many calories your body burns a day.
